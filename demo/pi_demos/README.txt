arucoOpenCV was strictly for proving that the camera can identify the pose & translation of the marker

openAL_demo was for proving that OpenAL could play sounds within 3d space.
Sounds could be moved with W A S D, and the user could be rotated within 3d space with Q and E.

IMU_OpenAL_Demo was for when the IMU was integrated with OpenAL through the teensy, it would prove that you could 
place a sound at a certain angle and then turn towards it with high precision
